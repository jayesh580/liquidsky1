ABOUT US

At LiquidSky Ventures Pvt Ltd, we aim to provide solutions for a cleaner environment. We advocate for the use of cutting-edge technologies to achieve environmental sustainability.


Our area of expertise is the integration of various water and air-based innovative technical solutions that are both sustainable and cost-effective. For our customers, we serve as a single point of contact for technology and service.


LiquidSky Ventures Pvt Ltd, founded by Sharat Nath, licences and supports pioneering technologies to create a better world. We provide multinational expertise by collaborating with European companies that are leaders in their respective fields

With a focus on water, we bring to India technologies that:


1. Treat industrial wastewater, recovering 95-98 percent of it for reuse in the manufacturing process and converting the remaining concentrate into a solid for disposal, achieving true ZERO LIQUID DISCHARGE (ZLD).
(Water Technology Solutions)

2. Generate a scalable amount of pure water from the atmosphere at a low cost.
(Air Technology Solutions)

3. Recovery of heat and energy to reduce carbon footprint (Heat Recovery Solutions)

4. Solution for digital contact tracing (Contact Tracing Solutions)

We bring Several Technologies to India with Water being our Essential Focus


LiquidSky Ventures Pvt Ltd offers a qualified team of engineers who can assist you in creating your ideal solution, onsite installation and training, as well as dedicated spare-parts and after-sales support.
